NAM LE is a passion project and the result of a sustainable and slow fashion vision.

Our mission is to minimize and eliminate textile waste — both preconsumer by using zero-waste pattern cutting, and post-consumer by using recycled or recyclable materials. We want to create conscious, zero-waste, and circular clothing collecons.

We truly believe that small, steady steps can lead to big changes.

— Nam Le

Designer and Founder
