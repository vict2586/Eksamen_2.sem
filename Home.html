Our garments are mindfully made by hand and with heart in our studio in Copenhagen.

Our mission is to minimize and eliminate textile waste — both pre-consumer by using zero-waste pattern cutting and post-consumer by using recycled or recyclable materials. We want to create conscious, zero-waste, and circular clothing collections.
